Who I Am

About Me

I am Chitty Babu M — an experienced trainer, technical facilitator, and community builder with over 20 years of expertise in teaching, facilitation, and the tech industry. I work extensively in both corporate and academic settings across India, transforming organizations and individuals through knowledge sharing.

My areas of specialization include Blockchain Technologies, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Business Analysis. I currently serve as a freelance trainer and facilitator, and as an IBM Subject Matter Expert (SME) providing deep insights into Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

As India Lead Ambassador for Celo Blockchain, I founded Celo IndiaDAO, authored governance proposals, and secured ecosystem funding — while conducting trainings, hackathons, and meetups for developers and students across India including rural areas.

I have been a mentor for international hackathons, conducted Faculty Development Programmes, and trained at prestigious institutions including VIT Vellore, VIGNAN University, Bharathidasan University, Titan Industries, and many more.
